Our work

We are...

A social movement

Our social movement seeks to engage with the South Asian community both virtually and in-person, raising awareness by sharing stories of members of our community and involving the community through active dialogue and education regarding mental health. By doing so, our movement hopes to normalize mental health challenges within our community, showing individuals currently struggling that they are not along and demonstrating to our community that these struggles do not diminish our strength, power, or resilience.

A non-profit organization

Our growing non-profit organization seeks to build access to South Asian mental health providers, expand our reach professionally, provide resources, and support our social movement. Though we do not directly provide clinical services, our non-profit is currently building a network of South Asian mental health professionals to whom, once complete, we can connect members of our community so that they have access to therapy that is culturally-competent and comprehends the unique stigmas and cultural pressures associated with mental health in South Asian communities.
