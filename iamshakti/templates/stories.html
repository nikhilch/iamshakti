Why we share

By sharing our stories, with their challenges and experiences, we hope to normalize mental health challenges within our community. We want to show individuals currently struggling that they are not alone and demonstrate to our community that these struggles do not diminish our strength, power, or resilience.

You can read some of our stories here, or if you feel comfortable, submit one of your own.
