Start a network

Local networks play a key role in delivering on I AM SHAKTI's vision - they are the primary way by which our social movement engages with the South Asian community on the ground and in person. Additionally, local networks organize events to spread our message and more intimately recognize the needs of their communities.

Furthermore, we hope that each network becomes a community for South Asians wherever you are - providing forums to meet, spaces to be vulnerable, and a family with the shared belief of the importance of mental health.

Share your Story


You are not alone.


By sharing our stories, with their challenges and experiences, we hope to normalize mental health challenges within our community. We want to show individuals currently struggling that they are not alone, and demonstrating to our community that these struggles do not diminish our strength, power, or resilience.

You can read some of our stories here, or if you feel comfortable, submit one of your own.
